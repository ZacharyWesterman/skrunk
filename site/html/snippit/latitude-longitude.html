To get the latitude and longitude of your location, go to Google Maps and search for your location, or click anywhere on the map. The latitude and longitude will be displayed in the search bar. You can then copy and paste them into the appropriate Lat and Lon fields.
Usually you would use your home location, as that's where you'll be most of the time, and doesn't change much.
Note that the weather alert system will never track where you actually are, instead it just checks the forecast at the chosen location.
