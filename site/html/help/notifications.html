Notifications

The following actions will trigger a push notification:

  • Extreme high/low temperatures in your area (if enabled for your account).
  • Someone comments on a bug report you created.
  • A bug report you created was marked as resolved.
  • Someone requested to borrow a book you own.

This is the current complete list, but it may change in the future. As new notification types are added or removed, this page will be updated to reflect that.

Important Notes:

Notifications follow certain guidelines to keep them as useful as possible.
  1. They should be infrequent. Any more than a few notifications per day is spam!
  2. They must only contain important information. Aim for concise and precise.
  3. No manipulative language. Our robots get really sad when we prod users for emotional response
If any of the above do not apply, contact an admin!
