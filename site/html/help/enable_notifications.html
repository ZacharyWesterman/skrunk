Enabling Notifications

iOS

If you have an iPhone or iPad running iOS 18 or later, you can enable push notifications in the Safari web browser.
  1. Open the Settings app.
  2. Scroll down to the Apps tab and click on it.
  3. Find Safari and click on it.
  4. Click on the Advanced tab at the bottom of the screen.
  5. Click on Feature Flags.
  6. Find the Notifications setting and enable it.

Android

Websites should prompt for notification access, but that feature may be disabled either by the user, or by default. You can easily enable it in Chrome.
  1. Open the Chrome browser.
  2. To the right of the address bar, click the three dots.
  3. Scroll down to Settings and click on it.
  4. Scroll to the bottom and click Site Settings.
  5. Find Notifications and click on it.
  6. At the top, enable the feature.
If you are using Firefox, the steps may be a little different.

PC

In or near the address bar, there should be icons indicating what features are enabled or disabled for this site.
  1. Click on those icons.
  2. Find the Notifications setting that says "blocked" or "disabled" or similar.
  3. Click on the X button to re-enable the feature for this site.
